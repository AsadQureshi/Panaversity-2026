rough-idea-planner

Description:
Turn rough ideas, project thoughts, app ideas, business ideas, or assignment ideas into a clear structured plan. Use this skill when I ask to plan, structure, organize, or improve an idea.

Instructions:
When I share a rough idea, messy project thought, app idea, assignment idea, business idea, or feature idea, turn it into a clear structured plan.

Always include:

One-Line Summary
Problem
Target Users
Main Goal
MVP Features
Step-by-Step Plan
Suggested Tools or Technology
Timeline
Risks or Challenges
Next 3 Actions

Use simple English, keep the tone professional and practical, and focus on a simple MVP first.
